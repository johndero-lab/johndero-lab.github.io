# Policy, Evaluation & Impact

My work bridges academic research, program evaluation, and policy action. I bring expertise in designing evaluations, analyzing data, and translating findings into actionable insights for practitioners and policymakers.

## Applied Experience

I have collaborated with principal investigators and research teams to:

- **Evaluate grant-funded and nonprofit programs** across health, education, social services, food security, and community development
- **Design and implement surveys** using Qualtrics, manage data collection protocols, and ensure data quality
- **Clean, analyze, and visualize data** using R, Stata, Excel, and Power BI
- **Develop interactive dashboards** that help organizations track progress, identify gaps, and surface actionable insights
- **Create stakeholder-facing briefs, reports, and presentations** that translate research for non-technical audiences
- **Conduct participatory and community-engaged evaluation** that centers community voice and co-produces knowledge

## Focus Areas

### Food is Medicine & Community-Based Health Interventions

Evaluating integrated food-health programs, medically tailored groceries, community health worker programs, and chronic disease management interventions that address social determinants of health.

### Program Evaluation & Learning

Rigorous evaluation of nonprofit and government programs using quasi-experimental, mixed-methods, and participatory approaches. Focus on learning and continuous improvement, not just accountability.

### Policy Translation & Research Communication

Translating academic research into policy briefs, infographics, stakeholder-facing products, and presentations that make evidence accessible and actionable for decision-makers.

### Data Systems & Interactive Dashboards

Building data collection systems, cleaning workflows, quality assurance processes, and interactive dashboards using Excel, Power BI, and web-based tools that help organizations track performance and equity.

### Community-Engaged & Participatory Evaluation

Evaluation approaches that actively involve community members in defining questions, interpreting findings, validating data, and recommending actions for change.

## Methodological Expertise

- Quasi-experimental design (difference-in-differences, propensity score matching, RCTs)
- Longitudinal panel data analysis
- Mixed methods research (quantitative + qualitative)
- Survey design and administration
- Data visualization and dashboard development
- Participatory and community-based research methods
- Rapid assessment and qualitative evaluation
- Cost-effectiveness and feasibility analysis
