# Research Themes

My research encompasses six interconnected areas:

1. **Social Protection and Resilience Governance** — How can formal and informal systems integrate to build resilience?

2. **Food Security and Livelihood Transformation** — How do policies shape food security and livelihood diversification in vulnerable communities?

3. **Community Wellbeing and Participatory Research** — How do communities define wellbeing, and how can participatory methods advance equity?

4. **Health Equity and Social Determinants** — How do social policies address health inequities rooted in broader determinants?

5. **Program Evaluation and Policy Analysis** — How can rigorous evaluation inform better policies and practice?

6. **Youth Development and Civic Engagement** — How do institutions support young people's agency and participation?

---

Current and recent work focuses on decolonial approaches to social protection, integrated food security and livelihoods programming, resilience in the Global South, and community-based health interventions.
