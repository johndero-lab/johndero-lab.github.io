# About Me

Sunday John Oluwadero holds a PhD in Education and Social Policy from the University of Delaware. His work sits at the intersection of community wellbeing and social policy, participatory and community-engaged research, social protection and resilience governance, health equity and social determinants of health, program evaluation, policy analysis, youth development, and civic engagement.

## Expertise

- Causal inference and quasi-experimental methods
- Difference-in-differences and matched designs
- Impact evaluation using longitudinal and survey data
- Mixed methods research
- Participatory and qualitative research
- Quantitative analysis in Stata and R
- Data visualization and dashboards
- Knowledge mobilization and stakeholder engagement

His work spans academic research, program evaluation, public health, community partnerships, and policy analysis across the US, UK, and Africa.
