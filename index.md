## About Me

I am a researcher and evaluator working at the intersection of social policy, resilience governance, community wellbeing, and program evaluation. My work combines causal inference, mixed methods research, and participatory approaches to examine how institutions, policies, and community systems shape food security, livelihood resilience, and equity in contexts of vulnerability.

**PhD in Education and Social Policy** from the University of Delaware (2025–2026), funded by the Doctoral Fellowship for Excellence.

My expertise spans causal inference, impact evaluation, mixed methods research, participatory approaches, quantitative analysis (R & Stata), and policy translation.
